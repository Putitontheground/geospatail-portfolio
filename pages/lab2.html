Lab 2 - Cartographic Design

Cartographic Design - Thematic Map

This is my submission for the thematic map, the topic I wanted to look at was the use of water on Marlborough farms and the general water deficit

The annual water deficit is based on a water balance model using monthly estimates of mean daily temperature, mean daily solar radiation, and mean rainfall, this layer is used as an indicator of soil dryness.

I was curious to see where water is used on farms in a region of the country that I knew were heavily irrigated, it followed the trend I was expecting. Another interesting pattern I noticed was that almost all of the irrigated land are vineyards.
