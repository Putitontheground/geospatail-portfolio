Mayfair Art Fair was a new and dynamic alternative art fair which presents emerging and unrepresented artists from Aotearoa New Zealand and the Pacific. In response to the pandemic we shifted the art fair to an online space.

My role in this project was to interpret each of the artists booth sketches and then 3D model, texture, animate, and render their artworks in the surreal spaces they had envisioned. Due to the nature of the 3D rendered scene the artists could take these ideas in any direction they pleased. Mayfair ran for 5 weeks from July 30th.

Role: 3D modelling, texturing, rigging, animation, lighting and rendering.
